About our Project:

Do you ever have a stroll one nice evening and you think to yourself, “Hey, that plant looks beautiful. But, what plant is it?” This is where Fun with Flora! ® comes into play. Point your camera at any plant, and get information within seconds, including its common name, scientific name, informational links, plant diseases and more!
